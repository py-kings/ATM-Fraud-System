How this is processed

  • 1
    Flask collects the form input and calls one stored procedure - WithdrawMoney(), DepositMoney(), or TransferMoney().
  • 2
    The procedure validates account status & balance, then updates Account and inserts into Transaction.
  • 3
    The LogTransaction trigger fires automatically and calls CalculateRisk() to score the transaction.
  • 4
    If the score is ≥ 70, the FraudDetection trigger writes a row into Fraud_Log - visible immediately on the Fraud Log page.
